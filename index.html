Daily Health Tips

🚶

Move Every Hour

A 5-minute walk every hour reduces cardiovascular risk and improves circulation significantly.

💧

Hydrate Proactively

Drink a glass of water before each meal. Mild dehydration impairs cognitive function by 20%.

😴

Sleep Consistency

Wake up at the same time every day — even weekends. This regulates your circadian rhythm.

🧘

Breathe Deeply

Box breathing (4-4-4-4) activates the parasympathetic system, reducing cortisol in minutes.
